General Terms of Use

Emotion Recognition Console

Please review how this interface works, what it captures, and its limitations before proceeding.

What the Interface Does

The console provides a multimodal emotion signal by analyzing typed or spoken text and optional facial expressions. It fuses these signals into a single state to demonstrate adaptive interface behavior.

What Is Captured

  • Text you type or speech transcripts produced by your browser.
  • Live camera frames processed locally to estimate facial expressions.

Retention and Access

  • No audio or video is stored by the server.
  • Text sent to the server is processed in memory and not saved.
  • Only your current browser session can access the data.

Limitations

  • Emotion detection is probabilistic and can be inaccurate.
  • Results are not medical or psychological assessments.
  • Lighting, accents, and background noise can reduce accuracy.
